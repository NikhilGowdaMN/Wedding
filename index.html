Our love story

First meet

Apr 2025

We met for the first time at an office cricket event. From the moment we started talking, there was an instant connection. After the game, I found myself asking her about her life, her family, and everything in between. Everything she shared felt like it was exactly what I had been looking for. It felt like fate brought us together, and I knew right then that she was someone very special.

First date

May 2022

After an Angular training session, I knew I just had to talk to her. So, when we both left the office at the same time, I took a different route to my PG, one where I could walk with her for a little while. We walked together to the bus stand, having a beautiful conversation along the way. It felt so natural and easy, like we’d known each other for much longer. After reaching the bus stand, we said our goodbyes, but that walk was a moment I’ll never forget.

Proposal

Apr 14 2023

I never thought I would love someone the way I love her. Though I never officially proposed, she made sure I did—multiple times! She playfully asked me to propose from the start, and each time, I hesitated. But on a bus ride to her house, she asked again, and this time, I couldn’t help but say, "Yes, of course!" It wasn’t about the words—it was about the love and connection we already shared. Technically, she proposed to me countless times, but that day felt like the real deal.

Enagagement

Feb 23 2025

Sometimes the most beautiful moments are the simplest ones. Our engagement, held in the heart of Bangalore, was not marked by surprises or extravagance—but by something far more special: complete acceptance, love, and joy from both of our families. With no obstacles in our path and hearts full of happiness, we celebrated the beginning of forever surrounded by close friends and loved ones who made the day truly memorable. It was a calm, perfect promise of all that’s to come.
